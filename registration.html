Registration

Please register via the ICALP/LiCS/FSCD registration form. One can register for the workshop only, or also join the ICAL/LiCS/FSCD conference.

  • The is an early bird discount until may 17th.
  • Please register before June 19th.
  • There is a discount for students.
  • There is a discount for EATCS/ACM/IEEE members.

Please visit the ICALP/LiCS/FSCD website for more information about the registration and local information. Do not hesitate to contact one of the organisers if you have questions.
